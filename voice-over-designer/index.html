VoiceOverDesigner

The essential tool to design, prototype and test accessibility of iOS-apps

Design UI that is accessible for blind people in a minutes

VoiceOver Designer is a macOS-app to design accessible layout for different kinds of accessibility technologies. Create the layout and open it at iOS-app VoiceOver Preview to hear your design.

Markup over a screenshot of your UI

Just draw rectangles to specify place of accessible elements. Yes, just so simple.

Describe elements

Text for an element's label will be recognized automatically, just add additional properties.

Setup adjustable elements

Sliders or carousels are adjustable elements in terms of VoiceOver. Their values can be adjusted by vertical swipe.

Prototype on your iPhone

Save the document to iCloud and test on a real device with help from Handoff. Turn on VoiceOver to hear the result.

This process so simple that full-screen adaptation requires just two minutes:

Prototype

You can open any .vodesign document in VoiceOver Preview app and check how blind people hear your interface.

The sample of screen without proper accessible layout: a lot of elements, no labels, the structure is miracle.

After adaptation we have reduced number of elements, all of them have labels, the app become easier to use by hearing.

A lot of samples

Couple of videos that you've seen are just a samples from the app. At the begging of your learning you can check samples of full-scenario adaptation for several apps. Also, on different languages.

Hint: save files to iCloud, then you will be able to open files by Handoff.

If you have great samples of accessible layout you can share it with others, just add samples to the repository. Your samples will be visible is the app and helps other people to better understand VoiceOver rules.

If you learn VoiceOver first time you can start from video "How to navigate your iPhone or iPad with VoiceOver". Other videos from playlist also great!

Send your design to developers

After prototyping you can send the document to developer. It's not so cool like link-for-everybody like a Figma, but you can attach file to task-tracker :D

But we have another offer - the book "About accessibility on iOS". It's on Russian, but we are working on translation and you can check code examples for free!

Dreams Roadmap

Fix some bugs

We have just released our application and of cause we have unexpected bugs. We have tested a lot, who knows what could happen with real people.

iPad's version

During the development process we understood that you can design and check the result on a single device – iPad! A lot of thing have already done, but we should tweak some few details for better experience.

Figma integration

It will be naturally for designers to add accessible layout not in separate application, but directly in Figma. We are going to to so and remove existence of separate files, everything should be on cloud.

Auto-tests for VoiceOver

Do not broke accessibility with new release is one of the main properties of accessible product. We would like to provide possibility to generate test cases directly from design and simplify automation as a result.

Art-boards

At this moment you can add layout just for single screen, but we would like to link several screens in single document and make interactive prototypes.

Full support for accessible technologies

The app is created around VoiceOver, but we are going to add support for other properties of Voice Control and Switch Control. Also, the app should be fully accessible for all people.

We have not dates for this features and it's just a feature plans, but with your help we certainly can achieve that. Tell your friends about application and we will see that we have created important project.
